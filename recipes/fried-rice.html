Fried Rice

Description

The recipe is a fried rice. Protein is chicken with fried eggs on top

Ingredients

  • Cooked, chilled rice
  • Eggs
  • Vegetables
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil

Steps

  1. Heat up the oil in the large pan or wok over medium-high heat
  2. Add the eggs and scrable until cooked
  3. Add garlic and vegetables.
  4. Add the scrabled eggs back in and combine everything.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, garnish with green onions
